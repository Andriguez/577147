Gran Canaria

Gran Canaria is a little great continent. A miniature world with a melting pot of diverse landscapes which seem to be taken from different countries. A tiny Eden in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean that wins over anyone who sets foot there. Without a doubt, it’s a great destination. behind the beaches you will find aboriginal silos on clifftops, a neogothic church in banana plantations, a hidden prehispanic city and a colonial district in the middle of traffic and Over two miles of tan sand beach will make this destination one of your favorite holiday paradises. more info

Aruba

Aruba is one of the Lesser Antilles islands located in the Southern Caribbean Sea. The island is located miles off the coast of Venezuela and on clear days it is possible to see Venezuela from the south-eastern side of the island. Aruba has something to offer for every family member, so young and old will have a great time on our island. Most resorts offer family activities and besides the beaches there are plenty of adventurous activities to entertain the kids more info

Cartagena

Cartagena is a Colombian city that is located on the shores of the Caribbean Sea, in the northwestern part of the South American continent. The city has several archipelagos and islands around that are paradises for true rest. This beautiful city brings together the charm of colonial architecture giving you the opportunity to learn about Cartagena’s architectural and cultural heritage, the excitement of a vivid night life, fascinating cultural festivals, and lush landscapes, to bring you the perfect vacation experience. The beaches beckon, inviting you to unwind and enjoy yourself in the refreshing breeze and warm waters of the sea.. more info
